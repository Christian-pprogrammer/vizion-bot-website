KEY FEATURES OF THE AI DIGITAL WALKING STICK

Vizion Bot develops an Artificial Intelligent Digital Walking Stick for visually impaired people, designed to enhance independence, safety, and quality of life. Equipped with advanced AI sensors, it scans surroundings to detect obstacles, height changes, and potential hazards, alerting users through vibrations and audio cues. Key features include:

Obstacle & Height Change Detection: Detects obstacles and sudden ground-level changes to help prevent accidents.

⁠⁠Voice-Activated Assistant & Navigation: Provides real-time navigation, directions, and location information via simple voice commands or a Bluetooth headset.

⁠Rechargeable Battery with Solar Backup: Ensures all-day use, with a built-in solar panel for outdoor power support.

Time & Info Access: Users can ask the stick for the current time or other essential information at the touch of a button.

GUIDELINES FOR USING THE ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENT DIGITAL WALKING STICK

Visually impaired people should follow these instructions when using the digital walking stick to ensure safety and optimal performance:

⁠Wear the elastic strap located at the top of the stick for secure handling.

⁠Turn on the stick by pressing the button on the left-side switch.

⁠Hold the stick in front of your foot while walking.

Obstacle Alerts: The stick will vibrate to indicate obstacles:

Low vibration: Detected at approximately 1 meter from obstacles.

High vibration with sound: Detected at 80–0 cm from obstacles.

⁠Fold the stick when entering cars or other transport to protect it.

⁠Turn off the stick when you reach your destination to save power.

⁠Voice-Activated Assistant & Navigation: Simply say commands like “Direct me to [location]” or “Find the nearest bus station.” Get turn-by-turn navigation and real-time info via voice feedback or a Bluetooth headset.

⁠Clock Feature: Press the button to ask the digital walking stick for the current time.
