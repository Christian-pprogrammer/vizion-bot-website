Let’s discuss about you project

WeASSIST is a service that allows visually impaired people to receive detailed visual assistance from our professionally trained agents. Users can connect directly with our agents through the WeWALK app to get live assistance with various tasks such as indoor navigation at airports, or providing visual descriptions of objects and text. We developed WeASSIST Professional Visual Assistance to be the ultimate solution for your accessibility needs.
