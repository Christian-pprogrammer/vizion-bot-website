About Vizionbot

Vizion Bot Ltd creates innovative digital walking sticks for visually impaired individuals. Our advanced stick features obstacle detection up to 1 meter, vibration alerts, solar-powered battery, and adjustable design for safe, confident mobility.

Company Profile

Vizion Bot Ltd, a private company with certification from the Rwanda Development Board (RDB), was established to address the mobility challenges faced by visually impaired people. Founded by Mr. BIZIYAREMYE Adrien, the company was inspired by his recognition of the need for a solution that enables visually impaired people to navigate independently, especially those who rely on guardians and miss out on educational and public service opportunities.

After extensive research and development, Vizion Bot Ltd reached its goal of creating the innovative digital walking stick in January 2023. This assistive technology aims to enhance independence, reduce reliance on guardians, and improve access to essential services for individuals with visual impairments.

Our Vision

At Vizion Bot Ltd, our vision is to empower visually impaired people across Rwanda and all of Africa by providing innovative digital walking sticks. We aspire to create a future where all visually impaired people can navigate their environments with confidence and independence.

We envision a world where visual impairment is no longer a barrier to accessing education, employment, and essential services. Through our cutting-edge assistive technology, we aim to bridge the gap between limitation and possibility.

Our Mission

At Vizion Bot Ltd, our mission is to help visually impaired individuals walk independently and confidently. By offering innovative digital walking sticks, we aim to boost their mobility and reduce reliance on guardians.

We are committed to developing and delivering high-quality assistive technology that transforms lives, promotes inclusion, and creates opportunities for visually impaired individuals to participate fully in society.
